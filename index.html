// Core Strategy

CryptoCred + GCR Ultra Ruleset

Every decision is grounded in proven crypto-native trading methodologies.

▲ Bull Trend

Momentum-driven entries on pullbacks. Look for higher highs, higher lows. RSI bouncing off 40-50 zone. MACD above zero line. Size up on high-confidence breakouts.

◆ Accumulation

Range-bound price action after a downtrend. Volume declining, volatility compressing. Build positions slowly on support tests. GCR contrarian principle: buy when others are bored.

▼ Distribution

Range-bound after an uptrend. Smart money distributing to retail. Reduce exposure, tighten stops. Watch for divergences between price and momentum indicators.

▼ Bear Trend

Lower highs, lower lows confirmed. RSI rejecting at 50-60. Short signals only on rallies into resistance. GCR contrarian: "Everyone is bullish? I'm out."
